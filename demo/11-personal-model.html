What is Personal Model?

Your Personal Model is NOT decision history—it's your enduring context: goals, constraints, values, life stage, risk tolerance. AI uses this to keep decisions coherent over time, ensuring recommendations align with who you are and what you're trying to achieve.

💎 Core Values (Decision Principles)
Your non-negotiable principles that guide all decisions
1
Transparent Communication
Radical honesty with team, investors, customers. No hiding bad news. Default to over-communication.
2
Customer-First Always
Every decision considers customer impact. Long-term customer success over short-term revenue.
3
Sustainable Pace
Build for marathon, not sprint. Protect team wellbeing. No burnout culture. Work-life integration matters.
4
Data-Informed, Not Data-Driven
Use data to inform, but trust intuition and qualitative signals. Numbers don't tell the whole story.
5
Speed to Learning
Bias toward action and fast iteration. Fail fast, learn faster. Avoid analysis paralysis.
6
Equity & Fairness
Fair compensation, transparent equity splits, inclusive culture. No bro culture or favoritism.

🤖 How AI Uses Your Personal Model
Real examples of context application
Example: Hiring Decision
VP Sales Candidate
Without Personal Model: AI recommends hiring based on credentials and market benchmarks.

With Personal Model: AI flags that hire violates "No Hiring Before Revenue Validates" constraint. Notes current $400K ARR doesn't justify VP-level sales hire yet. Suggests fractional VP or SDR first to prove model. Aligns with conservative people risk tolerance.
Example: Fundraising Decision
SAFE vs Priced Round
Without Personal Model: AI compares SAFE vs priced round mechanics neutrally.

With Personal Model: AI considers "Transparent Communication" value and "18-month runway" constraint. Notes SAFE's valuation ambiguity conflicts with transparency value. Calculates runway impact of different structures. Recommends priced round despite complexity because it aligns with your values.
Example: Product Decision
New Feature vs New Product
Without Personal Model: AI evaluates market opportunity and technical feasibility.

With Personal Model: AI immediately flags "Single Product Focus Until $5M ARR" constraint. Recommends doubling down on core product features over shiny new product ideas. Suggests quarterly review when approaching $5M threshold.
