About Confidence Thresholds
These settings control when AI will make strong recommendations vs flagging decisions for additional review. Lower thresholds = more AI recommendations, higher thresholds = more human review required.

AI will only make strong recommendations when confidence exceeds this threshold. Below this level, decisions are flagged for human review.
