Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Important Notice: CID-AI is currently a working interactive prototype for evaluation purposes. This privacy policy describes our intended data handling practices for future production deployment.

1. Overview

CID-AI is committed to protecting your privacy and handling data with transparency. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information in the context of decision intelligence services.

2. Information Collection

Prototype Phase (Current)

During prototype evaluation, we collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, organization (if voluntarily provided via forms)
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, demo interactions (anonymized analytics)
  • Feedback: Comments and evaluation notes you choose to share

Production Phase (Planned)

In production deployment, we would collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email, organization, role
  • Decision Data: Information you input for decision analysis
  • Personal Decision Model (PDM): Preferences, objectives, constraints, and decision history you choose to store
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the system (for improvement purposes)

3. How We Use Information

Information would be used exclusively for:

  • Providing decision intelligence services to you
  • Building and maintaining your Personal Decision Model (if enabled)
  • Improving system accuracy and reasoning quality
  • Communicating about system updates and features
  • Ensuring security and preventing misuse

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your decision data for marketing purposes
  • Share your Personal Decision Model with others
  • Train AI models on your private decision data without explicit consent

4. Data Security

Production deployment would include:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Audit Logs: Complete record of data access and modifications
  • Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade cloud hosting with security certifications

5. Data Retention

  • Decision Data: Retained as long as your account is active, or as required by law
  • Personal Decision Model: Under your control; can be exported or deleted at any time
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data may be retained for system improvement

6. Your Rights

You would have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Export: Download your decision data and PDM in portable format
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable specific features like PDM learning
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information

7. AI and Decision Data

CID-AI's AI components process your decision data to provide reasoning chains and recommendations. Key principles:

  • AI processing happens within your secure account context
  • Your decision data is not used to train models for other users
  • Personal Decision Model remains private to your account
  • You can review and challenge any AI-generated analysis

8. Third-Party Services

Production deployment may integrate with:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosting and data storage
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage tracking
  • Security: Authentication and monitoring services

All third-party providers would be vetted for security and privacy compliance.

9. Compliance

CID-AI is designed to support compliance with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • SOC 2 security standards (planned)
  • Industry-specific regulations as applicable

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the system evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance of updates.
